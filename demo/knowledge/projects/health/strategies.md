# health — strategy

*Last reviewed 2026-04-19*

## Where we're going

Sustainable strength training (3x/week, year-round, no more 8-week
hiatuses), sleep average ≥7.5h, vitamin D in range, lab work twice
yearly. Health treated with the same rigor as work or family decisions.

## Active hypotheses

### H1: 7.5h sleep correlates with better workout performance

Hypothesis (logged 2026-04-10): nights with 7.5+ hours of sleep produce
~8% better next-day workout RPE compared to 6.5-7.5h nights.

**Status:** 12 days of data so far. Trend visible but small sample.
Will revisit at 30 days with sleep + post-workout RPE notes.

### H2 (active 2026-04-17): Saturday-morning workouts beat Friday-afternoon during client EOW weeks

Hypothesis: in any week with active client deliverables, Friday-
afternoon workouts will get displaced. Saturday morning is the right
default. (See hub `incidents.md` and `concepts/morning-routine.md` for
the cross-project context.)

**Status:** new — first Saturday workout 2026-04-19, second 2026-04-26
upcoming. Will review at 4 weeks.

### H3 (active): Conservative ramp on rebuild — 4 weeks of consistency before progressing weights

Hypothesis: returning from an 8-week break, the win is showing up 3x/week,
not lifting heavier each week. Stable load for 4 weeks builds the habit;
progress comes after.

**Status:** week 1 of 4 (started 2026-04-19). Lifts will hold at 75kg
squat / 95kg deadlift through 2026-05-19, then progress.

## Retired hypotheses

### H4 (retired 2026-04-04): Friday afternoon as the protected workout slot

Hypothesis from Q1: Friday 17:00-18:00 as the protected third workout
slot.

**Status retired:** 3 of 6 Friday workouts skipped. Friday afternoons
are *not* a protected slot in practice — client EOW pushes consistently
displace them. Replaced by H2 (Saturday morning).

## Principles

- **Strength training takes calendar priority equal to client work.**
  Logged in this strategy file because it has been violated and needs
  to be re-asserted.
- **No skipping two sessions in a row.** Skipping one is a signal;
  skipping two becomes a pattern.
- **Sleep is non-negotiable below 6.5h three nights running.** That's
  a calendar restructuring conversation, not a "tough it out" week.
- **Lab work twice a year, doctor visit once a year — recurring tasks,
  not optional.**
