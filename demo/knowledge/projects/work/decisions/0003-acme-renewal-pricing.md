# Decision 0003 — Acme renewal pricing

## Status: decided 2026-04-21

Going into the 2026 renewal at **+18% on the new SOW**, with scope
tightening (dropping the analytics add-on that was used twice all year).

## The question

After 12 months on the same MRR ($8,400), what's the right pricing for
the 2026 renewal SOW?

## Alternatives considered

| Option | Pricing | Scope | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| A. Hold flat | $8,400/mo | unchanged | Underprices the 18 months of accumulated context, but lowest renewal risk. |
| B. +10% | $9,240/mo | unchanged | Below industry inflation + skill premium; defensible but conservative. |
| **C. +18% (chosen)** | **$9,912/mo** | **drop analytics add-on** | **Higher than market inflation; justified by 18mo context + value delivered + Q1 strategy tightening to enterprise-SaaS focus.** |
| D. +25% | $10,500/mo | drop analytics + cap quarterly hours | Aggressive; would test the relationship without strong value pitch ready. |

## Rationale (option C)

1. **Pricing Creativity insight:** anchor first, justify after. SOW v2
   leads with "+18%" on page 1; justification on page 2. Tests
   hypothesis H2 in [`projects/work/strategies.md`](../strategies.md).
2. **Sarah's preference:** she's an anchor-on-numbers decider (see
   [`people/sarah-mendez.md`](../../../people/sarah-mendez.md)). Going
   in with the number is more efficient than building up to it.
3. **Scope tightening:** dropping the analytics add-on (2 uses in 12
   months) sharpens the proposal. We're billing for what we actually
   do.
4. **Strategic alignment:** Q1 data (decided 2026-04-13) showed
   enterprise-SaaS clients pay ~40% better. +18% on Acme moves us
   closer to that benchmark, not yet at it.

## Conditions for re-evaluation

- If Sarah counters at +10% or below: re-open the conversation,
  consider option B with same scope tightening.
- If Sarah accepts without negotiation: log as data point that we
  could have gone higher next time.
- If renewal closes at +18%: validate H2 (anchor-first pricing) at
  90-day check-in with Beta-Corp engagement (where we used the
  reverse pattern in 2025).

- 2026-04-30: target close date
