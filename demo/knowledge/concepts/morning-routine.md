# Morning routine

A cross-project concept that lives at the hub level because it touches
both [`family`](../projects/family/state.md) and
[`health`](../projects/health/state.md). The morning hour 6:30-8:00 is
the most-contested time in the demo "user's" week — kids need
breakfast, lunches packed, transport to school; the personal exercise
window also wants to live there.

## The conflict

Two activities are competing for the same 90-minute window:

- **Family logistics** (immovable): kids fed, dressed, bags packed,
  transport to school. Window: 7:00-8:00.
- **Personal exercise** (movable but with consequences): 30-45 min
  strength session. Window: anywhere 6:00-8:30.

When this is unresolved, exercise reliably loses (because family
logistics are immovable) and the workout gets pushed to "tonight" —
which then loses to client EOW pushes. This was the root cause of 3
missed Friday workouts in 6 weeks (see hub `incidents.md`).

## The current design

Per [`projects/family/state.md`](../projects/family/state.md), Tom is
moving to school bus from September. That frees the 7:30-8:00 driving
slot. The plan is to repurpose this window for a 30-min exercise
session three times a week.

Until September: workouts happen Saturday morning 7:00-8:00 (logged in
[`projects/health/state.md`](../projects/health/state.md) as the
Saturday-morning default).

## Why this is a concept (not a project decision)

Because it sits at the boundary of two projects and the resolution
involves trading time across them. A decision recorded only in family
or only in health would lose the cross-project rationale. By keeping
it here at the hub level, both project pages link to a single source
of truth.
